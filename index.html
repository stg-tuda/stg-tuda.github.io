Research Topics

Our research brings together AI, software engineering, and programming language design and implementation. We develop models, metholds and tools for more efficient, reliable and secure software systems.

Courses

We offer courses in programming languages, software engineering, and AI for coding assistance. Thesis supervision is available for TU Darmstadt students only.

Thesis Opportunities

We supervise Bachelor and Master theses on multiple topics in Programming Languages and AI/Software Engineering. Thesis topics are arranged individually by discussion with a researcher. Supervision is only possible for students of the TU Darmstadt.
